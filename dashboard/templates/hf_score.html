The interactive visualization above shows the overall “Human Freedom” score for each country, as calculated by the CATO institute, for each year since 2008. This score is indicative of the overall freedom experienced by citizens within these countries, calculated from how they scored across subcategories within the domains of “Personal” and “Economic” freedom. The names of these subcategories can be found in the drop-down menu of the settings page, which can be viewed in relation to military expenditure on the Military Expenditure page.
